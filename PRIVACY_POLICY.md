# Privacy Policy for Ludo Master

**Last Updated:** June 14, 2026

Ludo Master is built by the Ludo Master Team ("we," "us," or "our") as a fast-paced multiplayer application. This service is provided to you at no cost and is intended for use as-is.

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

By downloading, installing, or using Ludo Master, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:
*   **Usernames and Profiles:** Guest profiles or custom names you create to identify yourself in multiplayer matches and on the Leaderboard.
*   **Device Identifiers:** Hardware models, operating system version, and unique device IDs to prevent abuse, enforce VIP features, and manage in-game economies.
*   **Gameplay and Performance Data:** Win/loss ratios, coins earned, levels gained, and general engagement telemetry.

The information we request will be retained on your device or stored securely on our backend servers (such as Google Firebase) for persistent cloud multiplayer matching.

---

## 2. Integrated Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party software development kits (SDKs) to facilitate advertisements, background cloud services, and push notifications. These external vendors may collect information used to identify your device:

*   **Google Play Services:** Facilitates cloud-saving, sign-in, and leaderboard sync.
    *   [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob):** Serves personalized or non-personalized advertisements within our shop and tournament hub.
    *   [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)
*   **Google Firebase (Analytics, Messaging, Firestore):** Powering real-time notifications, local-to-cloud matchmaking, chat lobbies, and crash analytics.
    *   [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

---

## 3. Storage and Data Retention

*   **Offline Profiles:** All matches, local coin economies, computer-bot results, and custom settings done in offline mode remain stored exclusively on your device within our secured SQLite Room Database.
*   **Online Matches:** Active multi-player chat channels, pending invite lobby codes, and transaction histories are written to standard, secure Firebase platforms and are automatically pruned after match completion.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

---

## 5. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

---

## 6. Access and Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account, leaderboard entries, and in-app profile. Please drop us an email with your unique Profile UID (which can be found in your Profile Settings hub inside the App) to initiate a full data deletion request.
